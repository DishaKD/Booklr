Frequently Asked Questions


How can I order?

When you find a book you want, you can add it to cart, after you should login and go through the ordering process.

Which payment methods can I use?

You can use Credit or Debit cards and Cash On Delivery.

How to contact customer service?

If you have question regarding our Booklr online book store (ordering, account questions, technical questions), please contact us through "Contact Us" page.

What are the terms and conditions?

You can see the terms and conditions here.

How do create an account?

Go to "Contact Us" page, After fill all the needed information and click "Sign Up". After submitting the form, your account will be confirmed.

How can I reset my password?

You can retrieve your password by clicking "forgot password?" Instruction on password reset will be send to your email.

How do I change my account details?

You can change all your information on your account. Go to "Login" page and log in, then click "My Account" and "Edit Account Details". Here you can change all your account information.

How about Shipping time?

Shipping time will be 3-7 working days for local shipping.

Can I return a product?

If you want to return a product, please contact us through "Contact Us" page.
